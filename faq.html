Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

General Questions

What are your business hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and major holidays.

Where are you located?

We're located in Fort Lupton, Colorado. Visit our contact page for directions and a map.

Do you offer custom milling services?

Yes! We can mill lumber to your exact specifications. Contact us to discuss your project requirements and get a quote.

Livestock Shavings

What types of shavings do you offer?

We produce premium wood shavings in several varieties: horse stall shavings (dust-free and highly absorbent), small animal bedding, and general-purpose shavings. All are made from clean, kiln-dried lumber.

How much do shavings cost?

Pricing varies based on quantity and delivery requirements. We offer competitive rates with volume discounts for bulk orders. Contact us for current pricing.

How are shavings packaged?

Our shavings come in compressed, easy-to-handle bales that expand when opened. This makes them convenient for storage and transport.

Are your shavings dust-free?

Our horse stall shavings are specially processed to minimize dust, making them ideal for horses with respiratory sensitivities. They're also virtually dust-free compared to standard shavings.

Ordering & Delivery

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by filling out our online order form, calling us, or visiting our mill in person. We'll confirm availability and provide a quote.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we deliver throughout the Fort Lupton area and surrounding regions (within 30 miles). Delivery fees vary based on distance and order size. Pickup is always free at our mill location.

What is your minimum order quantity?

We don't have a strict minimum order, but delivery is most cost-effective for larger orders. Small orders are welcome for pickup at our mill.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders can be delivered within 2-3 business days. Rush delivery may be available for an additional fee depending on our schedule.

Lumber Products

What types of lumber do you sell?

We offer dimensional lumber (2x4, 2x6, 4x4, etc.), rough-cut lumber, specialty lumber, timber framing beams, and custom-milled products. Multiple wood species are available.

Is your lumber locally sourced?

Yes, we prioritize locally sourced lumber from sustainably managed forests whenever possible. We're committed to environmental responsibility.

Do you offer kiln-dried lumber?

Yes, kiln-dried lumber is available. Ask about kiln-drying services for custom orders.
